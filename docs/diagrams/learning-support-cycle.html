The learning support cycle

1

School connects

Manage the school accounts and see the assigned teacher, enrolled student, and linked parent.

  • Membership and relationships
  • Family language
  • No raw student artifact or transcript
2

Teacher publishes

Review the learning goal, sequence, likely misconceptions, quick check, family bridge, and agency move.

  • Grounded AI draft or reviewed fallback
  • Human decision before release
  • Choose support and scaffold level
3

Student creates

Choose a maker path, create a first design, critique it, revise it, and complete a final math self-check.

  • The learner owns the artifact
  • AI never sees or writes the artifact
  • Can challenge the record
4

Family responds

Read the teacher-approved summary, invite the learner to show the work, and return one bounded signal.

  • No raw student artifact
  • No model transcript
  • No private teacher notes
