# Adult reviewer kit

Use synthetic profiles only. Do not enter a real child's name, school, location, contact detail, health detail, or story. This is a usability and content review, not a test of educational effectiveness.

## Before the session

- Confirm the reviewer is at least 18.
- Explain that Kanni is an independent concept demo, not a school product.
- Ask permission before using any observation in the submission.
- Start from a clean same-device demo record.
- Record only task time, one issue, one change, and one permission-safe observation.

## Kerala teacher task

Complete the Class 1 activity, review it in Teacher mode, choose a strategy, and inspect the parent handoff.

Check:

- Is the addition content accurate and age appropriate?
- Does "In this activity" avoid diagnosing the learner?
- Are the three strategy choices useful and bounded?
- Is any curriculum-alignment claim justified? Until approval, Kanni does not use "SCERT-aligned" as a product claim.

## Parent task

Open the prepared Parent view without help. Explain what happened and what you would try at home.

Check:

- Is the activity summary clear?
- Is the home prompt simple enough to try once?
- Is it clear that this is not a score, rank, or diagnosis?
- Can the task be completed with low digital confidence on a phone?

## Recent adult learner task

Only use a recent Kerala learner who is at least 18. Complete the Class 11 reviewed-question path and locate the source card and Trust page.

Check:

- Is the linear-search explanation clear?
- Is the difference between a reviewed answer and supervised AI visible?
- Is Deep Check described as a bounded check rather than proof?

## Native Malayalam review

Review every fixed Malayalam string and the Malayalam golden answers in `lib/lessons.ts`.

Check:

- Natural wording and age fit
- Correct glyph rendering
- No artificial letter spacing
- Correct English-Malayalam mixing
- Consistent terms across the activity and lesson pack

Malayalam remains labelled preview until this review passes.
